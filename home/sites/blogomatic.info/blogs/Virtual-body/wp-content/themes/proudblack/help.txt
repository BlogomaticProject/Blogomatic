Lifestream Plugin Install.

1. Unzip the contents of the Plugins Folder.
2. Copy the lifestream folder in your Wordpress plugins dir. (wp-content/plugins)
3. Activate it
4. From your Wordpress Dashboard select which feeds do you want to appear in the footer area .

Important : Only the last 2 items are shown .



Twitter and Flickr Configuration

Enter your usernames for flickr and twitter in the Proudblack Options area.
